about: Cuddles



The artist behind arts by: Cuddles is currently a student at Bicol State College of Applied Sciences and Technology, pursuing a BSEMC-DAT course. With a deep passion for digital arts, they explore the realms of imagination and bring them to life through their creative works. Their dedication to the craft is evident in their attention to detail and vibrant use of colors.

As a fun fact, the name "Cuddles" draws inspiration from a character in the show "Happy Tree Friends." It adds a touch of whimsy and playfulness to the artist's identity, reflecting their ability to infuse joy and lightheartedness into their artistic creations.
